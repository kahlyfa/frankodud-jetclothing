Who We Are

We dress men who understand that how you show up matters.

FRANKO CLOTHING is a premium menswear brand rooted in the DMV — Washington D.C., Maryland, and Virginia. We curate clothing and accessories that carry weight: the weight of craftsmanship, of intention, and of commanding respect.

From boardroom-ready suits to distinguished walking staffs, every product in our collection is selected with one man in mind — the man who never settles.

The Founder

A man with a vision
and the discipline to execute it.

Franko Bud'jet didn't start a clothing brand. He built a philosophy and put fabric to it. Growing up in the DMV, Franko understood early on that how a man dresses signals how he sees himself — and how the world treats him in return.

After years of struggling to find pieces that matched his taste and standard, Franko decided to curate his own. He sourced premium suits, hand-selected every shirt, and tracked down craftsmen who made walking staffs worthy of the men who would carry them.

"Every man I've ever admired — the ones who moved rooms when they walked in — they all had one thing in common: they dressed with intention."
— Franko Bud'jet, Founder

Today, FRANKO CLOTHING serves discerning men across the DMV and beyond, becoming the trusted name for gentlemen who refuse to be ordinary. The brand is more than a store — it is a standard.
